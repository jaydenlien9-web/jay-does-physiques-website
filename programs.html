FIFO Packages

Training programs built around your roster

What you get

  • Two programs: one for site, one for home
  • Adjustments based on your roster and recovery
  • Equipment flexibility for site gyms
  • Check-ins when you're back in town
  • Remote support while you're away

Who it's for

Anyone working FIFO who wants to stay consistent despite the rotating schedule. Your program accounts for travel fatigue, site gym equipment, and the reality of shift work.

Why it works

FIFO schedules mess with normal training programs. You need something that adapts to your roster, not the other way around. I've worked with FIFO clients enough to know what actually works.
